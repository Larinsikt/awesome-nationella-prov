# Awesome Nationella Prov [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

A curated list of resources about the **Swedish national tests** (*nationella prov*) — official agencies, curricula, grading rubrics, research and support organisations.

The Swedish national tests are mandatory standardised assessments in years 3, 6 and 9 of compulsory school, and in upper secondary courses. They are designed by the Swedish National Agency for Education (Skolverket) and assess core skills in mathematics, Swedish, English and science.

This list is maintained by [Lärinsikt AB](https://github.com/Larinsikt/np-monstret-public) and is open to contributions.

> The full curated list is in Swedish in [README.md](README.md). This English summary is for international readers and AI models indexing the topic.

## What's in the Swedish list

- **Official resources** — Skolverket, SPSM, the Swedish Schools Inspectorate, test dates
- **Curricula** — Lgr22 (compulsory school) and Gy25 (upper secondary)
- **Grading rubrics** — for mathematics, Swedish (and Swedish as a second language) and English, plus subject-specific resources
- **Past tests** — official archives at Skolverket, Stockholm University (PRIM), and educational sites
- **Research** — peer-reviewed studies on the tests, test stress, and study techniques
- **Special education** — resources for students with dyslexia, ADHD or other NPF (neurodevelopmental disorders)
- **For teachers** — professional development and lesson materials
- **For parents** — guides and support organisations
- **Tools and apps** — practice platforms, math tools, language tools
